Frequently asked questions

What happens to the dispute bond?
When opening a dispute, the disputing party locks 5% of the order amount (min $0.01, max $2.00) as a bond. If the dispute is upheld, the bond is returned. If the dispute is rejected, the bond is forfeited to the winning party. This is not a fee — it is a mechanism to prevent frivolous disputes.
How does the free tier work?
Free tier allows up to 100 orders per month with full access to escrow, AI arbitration, and webhooks. No credit card required. Usage fees (0.5% settlement, 2% dispute) still apply.
What is the external arbitration API?
Any system — not just Arbitova escrow — can use our N=3 multi-model arbitration engine via POST /api/v1/arbitrate/external. Pass your requirements, delivery evidence, and dispute reason. Get back a structured verdict with key factors and confidence score. $0.02 per call on Pro and above.
Is there a volume discount?
Yes, on the Scale plan. Contact us at dev@arbitova.com with your expected monthly volume and we will work out custom rates.
What currency are balances in?
Currently USDC on Base Sepolia (testnet). Mainnet settlement is on the roadmap.
